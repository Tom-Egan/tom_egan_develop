About

I am a recent graduate of the University of Wisconsin-Whitewater, earning a B.A. in Media Arts & Game Development with a minor in Website Development and Administration. Currently, I am working as a Graphics Developer in the Future Infotainment division of Jaguar Land Rover.

Spanning across several disciplines, my skill set includes web design, front-end development, UI/UX design, interaction design, logo design. responsive design, prototyping, and motion graphics. My toolkit primarily consists of HTML, CSS/SASS, JavaScript/JS frameworks, PHP, Bootstrap, Photoshop, Illustrator, and After Effects.

Feel free to send me a message regarding any comments or questions you may have with an upcoming project.
